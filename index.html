Signature Campaign for Fair, Reasonable, and Equitable Pension

Legislative Proposal

"AMENDING R.A. 8291 to include, among others, the provision: 'Monthly pension will automatically increase whenever there is an increase in the salary of the position at which the member retired'; in order to correct the unjust difference between the retirement benefits package granted to ordinary government employees versus those in the judiciary, Office of the Ombudsman, National Prosecution Service and AFP."

Current Laws on Automatic Pension Increase

  • RA 9946: Automatically increases pensions of retired judiciary members.
  • RA 11059: Applies salary upgrades to eligible Ombudsman officials and employees.
  • RA 10071: Automatically increases pensions of retired prosecutors in the National Prosecution Service.

Who Is Covered?

  • RA 9946: Justices and Judges, and their surviving legitimate spouses.
  • RA 11059: Ombudsman, Deputies, Special Prosecutor, legal employees from SG 26‚Äì29 with certain qualifications.
  • RA 10071: Officers and members of the National Prosecution Service.

Why This Is Unfair

The current laws exclude rank-and-file employees and most government executives. We have served the public with dedication and yet are denied the automatic pension increases enjoyed by a few. This disparity is:

  • Unfair: We already earn and receive less, yet we face the same cost of living.
  • Unreasonable: Retired positions should not define post-retirement worth.
  • Discriminatory: It divides retirees into privileged and forgotten classes.
  • Demoralizing: It kills the motivation of those still in service and nearing retirement.
